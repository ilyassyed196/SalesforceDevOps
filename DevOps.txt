Salesforce DevOps Training in Techzy Consulting, Explaining the concept of merging in Salesforce DevOps, which is the process of combining code changes from different branches into a single branch. This is typically done to integrate new features, bug fixes, or improvements into the main codebase. Merging helps to ensure that all changes are consolidated and can be tested together before being deployed to production. It is an essential part of the DevOps workflow, allowing for collaboration and continuous integration among development teams.
